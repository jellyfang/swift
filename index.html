FAQs about Levi's Stadium in Santa Clara

  • Where is Levi's Stadium located? It's located in Santa Clara, California, in the heart of Silicon Valley.
  • What are the transport options? The stadium is accessible via car, light rail (VTA), Caltrain, BART, bus, bicycle, and on foot. Parking options need to be booked in advance from the Levi's Stadium website.
  • What's the bag policy? Levi's Stadium follows a strict bag policy in line with NFL guidelines. Bags should be clear plastic or clear vinyl, and not be more than 12" x 6" x 12". Small clutch bags approximately the size of a hand are allowed.
  • Can I bring food or drinks? Outside food and beverages are generally not permitted, except for bottled water in factory-sealed, plastic bottles that are 20 oz. or less in size, and medically necessary items.
  • Are cameras allowed? Non-video cameras are generally permitted for football games and stadium events; however, professional cameras (lenses six inches or over) are not allowed. Camera policies may vary according to the event.
  • What about accessible seating? Levi's Stadium offers wheelchair-accessible seating areas and locations for guests with mobility impairments.
  • Can I smoke in the stadium? Levi's Stadium is a smoke-free facility. There are no designated smoking areas.
  • What time do gates open? Gates usually open two hours prior to event start time, but this can vary by event.
